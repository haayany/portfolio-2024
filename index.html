Dani is 

Researching

Develop a deep understanding of users, industry dynamics, and product specifics to inform the design process.

Initiate the project by collecting qualitative and quantitative data through user interviews, market analysis, and competitive research. This phase aims to build a solid foundation that guides the rest of the project.

Identifying

Pinpoint and articulate core challenges from multiple perspectives to direct the design strategy.

Analyze the data gathered during the research phase to identify underlying user needs and potential barriers. Prepare a detailed report that highlights these findings, ensuring a clear path forward for ideation and design.

Designing

Transform insights and identified needs into tangible design solutions.

Create a series of mock-ups and prototypes that address the challenges outlined in the issue report. Iterate on these designs through internal reviews and peer feedback to refine the concepts.

Communicating

Enhance and validate designs through continuous engagement with stakeholders and technical teams.

Conduct ongoing discussions with users and stakeholders to gather feedback and further refine the design. Collaborate closely with engineering teams to ensure technical feasibility and alignment with product goals.
