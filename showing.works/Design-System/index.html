Design System

UX Design, UI Design, Research

2022-2024

 
Overview

The company had three different channels conducting business. Retail, Consumer-direct, and wholesale. B2C, retail and consumer-direct part of the company had multiple subsidiaries that require different branding. Similarly, wholesale customers wanted to apply their own branding to our provided services as well.

Another issue with their proprietary system was front-end development speed. There was no guideline nor component collections even for a button. Every buttons on different pages were slightly different.

 
Component & Semantic Tokens

Figma didn’t have a native way to apply tokens properly but with a limited library features. I was able to make a decent way to apply different themes with library swap feature, but it was still too much to ask new designers who are not familiar with Figma.

Thankfully, Figma introduced variable feature. And I was able to update all the components with it quickly.

Fun story: Few days after the update, our tech-savvy CEO heard about it, and my manager was able to say, “we are already using it.”

While making and applying component tokens, I realized two things; In code, token naming convention is not consistent It’s a good time to apply Semantic Tokens

I grabbed front-end engineers and showed possible two naming conventions, suggestion for applying semantic tokens for actions. Thanks to the great front-end engineering team, we connected Figma tokens to the code. Figma token update means code is updated automatically.

 
Product for Our Team

I believe making a design system is making a product for designers and software engineers. So I did everything I could to make it convenient for them. Slot and table component is some of the my efforts to make that possible.

With Slot component, designers could update the contents inside of a component without disconnecting its library. (slot component concept is originally from Atlassian)

Working on a financial product meant a lot of tables. (or similar structure) Designers always needed to spend a lot of time to move around table borders, cells, rows, and columns. After the update, designers never needed to work more than 5 minutes on any kind of table updates.

 
Outcome

The design system helped us to kickstart and release two proprietary products within a month and two months. Three more products were designed with it.
