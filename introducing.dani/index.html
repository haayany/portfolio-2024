Happy that you are here!

I am Dani, a UX Designer passionate about crafting interfaces that captivate and engage. Much like the immersive worlds of a gripping novel, a cinematic masterpiece, or a meticulously designed game, I believe that everyday digital experiences should be constructed intentionally. With a keen eye for detail and creativity, I approach design as an art form, striving to blend functionality with aesthetic pleasure.

My curiosity and enthusiasm for understanding the 'why' behind user actions drive my design process. I am fascinated by the psychology of decision-making and the diverse motivations that guide human interaction with technology. This curiosity fuels my ability to overthink and envisage numerous scenarios, ensuring that the products I design are not only visually pleasing but also intuitively aligned with user needs, behaviors, and stakeholders' goals.

Went through various work cultures and company sizes—from small businesses to medium-sized enterprises — I've gained a wide range of experiences that shape my approach to design. My journey through industries like video game design, digital marketing, sales, web design, and compliance has equipped me with a versatile skill set and a broad perspective.
